FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What services does the medical clinic offer? The medical clinic offers a wide range of radiology services including X-rays, ultrasounds, CT scans, MRI scans, and mammograms.
Do I need a referral from a doctor to schedule an appointment? Yes, a referral from a doctor is required to schedule an appointment for radiology services at our clinic.
How long does it take to get the results of my radiology test? The turnaround time for radiology test results may vary depending on the type of test. Generally, you can expect to receive your results within a few days.
Can I request a copy of my radiology images? Yes, you can request a copy of your radiology images. Please contact our clinic's reception desk for more information on how to obtain your images.
Does the clinic accept insurance for payment? Yes, the clinic accepts most major insurance plans. However, it is recommended to contact your insurance provider to confirm coverage for radiology services at our clinic.
